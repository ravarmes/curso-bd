Práticas 4.5 — DQL: Consultas e Ordenações

Estrutura do SELECT

SELECT col1, col2
FROM tabela
WHERE col1 = 'valor'
  AND col2 LIKE '%texto%'
  AND col3 IN (1, 2, 3)
  AND col4 BETWEEN 10 AND 100
ORDER BY col1 ASC;

WHERE filtra linhas. Operadores: =, <>, LIKE, IN, BETWEEN, IS NULL. ORDER BY ordena ASC/DESC.
